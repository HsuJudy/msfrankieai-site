Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026

Ms. Frankie AI ("Ms. Frankie," "we," "us," or "our") operates msfrankieai.com and provides an AI voice receptionist service for dental practices. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through this website and our related services, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Ms. Frankie is designed for the marketing, scheduling, and communications layer of a dental practice. Protected health information (PHI) that a clinic entrusts to us in the course of providing our service is handled under a separate Business Associate Agreement (BAA) with that clinic, in accordance with HIPAA. This website itself collects business and contact information, not patient medical records.

Information we collect

  • Information you give us when you book a demo, call our line, or contact us — such as your name, practice or clinic name, email address, phone number, the practice-management software you use, and number of locations.
  • Communications — the content of calls, messages, and emails you exchange with us, including recordings of demo calls where disclosed to you.
  • Usage information — basic technical data automatically collected when you visit the site, such as your browser type and pages viewed.

How we use information

  • To schedule and conduct demos, and to respond to your questions.
  • To provide, maintain, and improve our service.
  • To send you service-related and, where permitted, marketing communications (you can opt out at any time).
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us operate — for example our customer-relationship and communications platform (GoHighLevel) and our website host (Netlify) — and only as needed to provide the service, or when required by law.

Data security & retention

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information, including encryption in transit and at rest and role-based access controls. We retain information for as long as needed to provide the service and meet legal requirements, then delete or de-identify it.

Your choices & rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and you may opt out of marketing messages. To make a request, email hello@msfrankieai.com. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the CCPA.

Children's privacy

Our website and services are intended for businesses and are not directed to children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.

Contact us

Questions about this policy? Email hello@msfrankieai.com.
