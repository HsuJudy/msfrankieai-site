FAQ

A few things you might be wondering

Will you work with our current phone system and software?

Yes. I connect to your existing phone line and I'm built to work with the major practice management systems — so there's nothing to rip out or replace.

How do you protect patient information?

I encrypt everything in transit and at rest, log every interaction, and limit access by role. I'm built around the privacy principles behind HIPAA and PHIPA.

How long until you're answering our calls?

Most practices have me live within days. My team handles the setup around your services, hours, providers, and booking rules.

What happens with urgent or complicated calls?

I recognize urgent situations and route or escalate them by your rules, and I always capture a detailed message so your team can follow up fast.
